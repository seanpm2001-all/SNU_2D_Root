PSY - GANGNAM STYLE(강남스타일) M/V

Video disabled in Vanilla mode Click here to watch

By: Psy

Uploaded to YouTube: July 15th 2012

File size: 86,211,090 bytes (86,211.09 Kilobytes) (86.21 Megabytes)

File format: MP4 (MPEG Part 14)

Duration: 4 Minute 12 seconds

Framerate: 24 frames per second

Download link: Click here to download

Significance:

This was the first video to exceed the maximum value of a 32 bit integer in views, and YouTube views were still being recorded by a 32 bit integer. This video raised the roof and forced YouTube to switch the view counter to a 64 bit integer. It was also the first video to reach 1 billion views
