Temporary ban of SNU in the Europeasn Union (EU)

Written by Sean P. Myrick on March 26th 2019

After debate in the European Union for almost a full year, the new internet law has been passed today. We are not giving up, and we found a workaround, but it doesn't work for everyone.

To protect SNU until Article 13 is gone, or the algorithm gets really good, we are imposing a ban on access to SNU for anyone in the European Union. This affects the following countries:

What does this mean for you

SNU hasn't evem launched yet. If Article 13 is still malicious and active, you will not be able to get into SNU without a VPN, and even with that, it's at your own risk. This means you won't be able to search for content, view unverified content, upload, comment, review, or report anything.

Will this ever get better?

We really hope so. We didn't want to have to censor our site from the world, but it is the only way.

No hate to the EU, just sort things out, then we can open the gate for you again.
