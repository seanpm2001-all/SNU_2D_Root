Notice about autoplaying videos, and looping

This is one of the only pages on this site that uses autoplaying videos. There is currently discussion on if autoplaying videos should be banned. The way I am using them here is not malicious at all, but a massive amount of the internet abuses the auto tag. The way it is being used here is to start a welcome video about what this mode is about, and that's it. Just a 12 second silent video telling you about this page. The functionality may be removed in the future if the auto tag is removed, however, the decision hasn't been made yet. Hopefully, the abusive auto tag users don't make it worse for the rest of us.
