Reviewing

Content reviewing is an important community tool. When SNU starts out small, we will give the option to get your content manually reviews.

Once content passes inspection, it can have reports disabled, and it will be categorized correctly.

Leaving reviews on content is helpful for people to know if a product is trustful or not. Please give valid criticism when leaving negative reviews. Shaming content without reason will result in a 6 week account suspension
